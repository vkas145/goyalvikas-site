🔒 Simple Privacy Policy
I am Goyal Vikas. I designed this quiz for my son and I'm sharing it just for learning purposes. There is no company, no product, no monetisation behind this page — just a dad trying to make practice fun for kids.
  • No sign-up, no login. Just pick a first name so scores don't mix up on a shared phone. No passwords, no email, no phone number is ever asked.
  • Nothing leaves your device. Your answers, your name and your scores all stay in localStorage inside this browser. No database, no server, no cloud sync, no backup anywhere.
  • Who can see your scores? Only whoever uses this browser on this device. Not the teacher, not the parent on another phone, not me, not anyone.
  • Analytics. We use Google Tag Manager for anonymous, aggregated page-view counts only — it records things like "someone in India opened Chapter 3", never your name or answers.
  • Deleting your data. Tap Reset on the scoreboard to wipe scores, or clear browser data in Settings. There is no "delete my account" form because there is no account.
  • Built for kids. Designed for 7-year-olds. No ads, no trackers beyond the analytics above, no links off to social media.
Questions? Reach out on goyalvikas.com. Made with ❤️ out of love for our kids — not for money, not for data.
